How frequently driver goes to Coffee House is the strongest predictor of whether they'll accept the coffee shop coupon Other factors include age, gender, and income

Weather (temperature) also has big effect on whether driver will accept coffee house coupon. Drivers are more likely to accept coupons when temperature is with very cold anor is very warm.

Three groups/conditions were analyzed:

1. Drivers who go to coffee shops more than once per month with annual income between  12,500− 49,000
2. Drivers who go to coffee shops more than once per month, who are female under the age of 31
3. Drivers who go to coffee shops more than once per month when the temperature is very low or very high

Group 2 is the most likely to accept the coffee shop coupon with 72% acceptance rate. Likelyhood of coffee shop coupon acceptance in groups 1 and 3 is also very high - 69%. All three targeted groups show much higher coffee shop coupon acceptance that the general population at 50%.
